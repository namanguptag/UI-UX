Frequently Asked Questions

What are the official dates for SATONOVA 2025?

SATONOVA 2025 is scheduled for **October 25th and 26th, 2025**. All major events and competitions will take place on these two days.

Who is eligible to participate?

The fest is open to all university and college students. You must present a valid student ID from your institution at the registration desk upon arrival.

How do I register for an event?

You can register for any event by visiting the Register page on our website. Simply fill out the form for the events you wish to participate in. On-the-spot registrations will be limited, so we recommend registering online in advance.

Is there a registration fee?

Yes, there are nominal registration fees for most competitive events to cover organizational costs. The fee for each event is listed on the Events and Registration pages. There is no entry fee for general visitors.

Will accommodation be provided for outstation participants?

Limited accommodation is available in the university hostels on a first-come, first-served basis. Please indicate your need for accommodation on the registration form. We will contact you with details if a spot is available.
